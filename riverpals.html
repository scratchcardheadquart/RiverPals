River Pals Sail Embark on an adorable and relaxing puzzle adventure with River Pals Sail! Help a variety of lovable animals board their boats and sail across the river. Match 3 of the same animal on the grid, and watch them happily hop onto the boat for their journey. Your mission is to assist all the animals in crossing the river safely!
